The forest under the management of Dongning Forestry Bureau is WWF priority working area in Amur tiger/leopard conservation.

Day 9: Snow Leopard camera trap

This extraordinary scene is from a new cache of video and photos taken through a camera trap study by the Royal Government of Bhutan and WWF in Wangchuck Centennial Park, the country's largest protected area.

The cameras filmed both predator and prey species, ranging from the Tibetan wolf to the musk deer, suggesting this protected area is a key refuge for Himalayan biodiversity.
